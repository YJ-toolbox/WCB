About Us

WhiteCollarBlue was established in 2010 by Grant Styles. Grant, a highly capable and results driven business manager, has demonstrated his expertise both in the Australian and New Zealand Business Solutions markets since 1992. Working across many market sectors Grant displays a natural enthusiasm and passion and is recognised for providing exemplary flexible "People Solutions That Work". With 20 years in the industry Grant's loyal customer base still enjoy the benefits that only a true personalised service brings.

To WhiteCollarBlue, service is first and foremost and we will strive to become a major influence in the growth and success of your business. WhiteCollarBlue was launched with one focus in mind, to cut through an already saturated recruitment market and position ourselves as the People Solutions provider of choice. We talk straight and act quickly through the delivery of tailored flexible services that support Temporary, Casual and the Permanent placement market.

With a personal approach and strong attention to detail, Grants team will provide both White collar and Blue collar people solutions that work, we will accomplish this through innovation in our practices - dedication in our service delivery and commitment to both our Candidates and Clients alike.

We are ambitious for our people and we are passionate about creating value for our Clients. We are able to offer practical advice backed by our industry knowledge. WhiteCollarBlue will epitomise the combined themes of Flexibility, Agility, Loyalty, Price, Relationships and Innovation in the provision of "People Solutions That Work".
